1. Introduction

Welcome to Laundry Buddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our laundry management service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Name: To identify you and personalize your experience
  • Email Address: For account verification, communication, and password recovery
  • Phone Number: Optional, for SMS notifications and account recovery
  • Address: To facilitate laundry pickup and delivery services
  • Password: Encrypted and stored securely for account access

2.2 Laundry Order Information

  • Order details (items, quantity, special instructions)
  • Order timestamps and status
  • Payment information (if applicable)
  • Delivery preferences

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: For authentication and preferences
  • Location Data: If you enable location services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Process laundry orders, track status, and communicate updates
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your account, handle authentication
  • Communication: Send order confirmations, status updates, and service notifications
  • Improve Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries and resolve issues
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

4.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with:

  • Cloud Hosting: MongoDB Atlas, Render.com (for data storage and hosting)
  • Email Service: Resend (for sending transactional emails)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (no personal data)
  • Payment Processors: If payment features are implemented

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Investigate security incidents or fraud
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Access Controls: Limited employee access to personal data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Database Security: MongoDB Atlas with encryption at rest
  • Session Management: Secure session handling with HttpOnly cookies

Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Update

You can access and update your personal information through your account profile page.

6.2 Data Export

You have the right to request a copy of your data. Contact us at privacy@laundrybuddy.com to request an export.

6.3 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by:

  • Using the "Delete Account" feature in your profile settings
  • Emailing us at privacy@laundrybuddy.com

Note: Some information may be retained for legal compliance or legitimate business purposes.

6.4 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing emails by:

  • Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
  • Updating your communication preferences in account settings

6.5 Cookies

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Most data deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data retained for compliance (e.g., transaction records)
  • Backup Data: May persist in backups for up to 90 days

  • Right to withdraw consent
